Features and Scope Of Airline Reservation System

Message:After booking is done,the Customer get details regarding the Flight through message.

Gmail:After booking is done,the Customer gets details regarding the Flight through mail on his registered email-id.

PDF:Once the ticket is confirmed, PDF is generated.

Ticket Cancellation :Auto-Increment in number of seats feature after the Ticket is Cancelled.
  
Ticket Booking:Auto-Decrement in number of seats feature after the Ticket is Reserved.

Flight Schedule:Customer can Search for a Flight and check the status and schedule regarding a particular flight.

Flight Search:Customer can search a flight.


Other Features:

1]On One Ticket multiple Passengers can reserve seats.
2]User Freindly Interface.
